## Introduction

Welcome to **Manga Reader App**! This application allows you to discover, read, and manage your favorite manga, manhwa, and manhua series directly on your device. With access to thousands of titles through MangaDex, you can easily browse recent releases, search for specific series, and build your personal library.

### Key Features
- Browse the latest manga series
- View detailed information about each series
- Read chapters online
- Search for specific titles
- Save your favorite series to a personal library
- Export and import your library

## How to Use

### Browsing Recent Series

The Home Page displays a curated list of recently updated manga series.

**Steps:**
1. Open the Manga Reader App
2. The Home Page automatically loads recent series
3. Scroll through the list to see more titles
4. Each entry shows:
   - Cover art

**Tip:** Pull down on the screen to refresh the list and see the latest updates!

### Viewing Series Details

Get comprehensive information about any manga series before you start reading.

**Steps:**
1. From the Home Page or Search Results, tap on any manga series
2. The Series Detail Page will open, displaying:

   - Detailed description/synopsis

4. Use the reader controls:
   - Top bar: Chapter information and exit button
   - Bottom bar: Page navigation

**Reader Tips:**
- Pinch to zoom on any page for better detail
- Double-tap to fit page to screen

- Tap the back button at the top to return to the Series Detail Page

### Managing Your Library

Save your favorite manga series to your personal library for quick access.

#### Adding Series to Your Library

**Steps:**
1. Navigate to any Series Detail Page
2. Tap the **"Add to Library"** button
3. A confirmation message will appear
4. The series is now saved to your local device

![Add to Library Button - Placeholder](https://blairydev.github.io/ase456-individual-project.io/images/library.jpeg)
*[IMAGE: Screenshot highlighting the "Add to Library" button on a series detail page]*

**What Gets Saved:**
- Manga ID
- Series title
- Description
- Cover art URL
- Author(s) and Artist(s)
- Tags
- Publication status

#### Accessing Your Library

**Steps:**
1. Tap the **Library** icon in the navigation menu
2. View all your saved manga series
3. Tap any series to view its details and chapters

![Library Page - Placeholder](https://blairydev.github.io/ase456-individual-project.io/images/library.jpeg)
*[IMAGE: Screenshot of the library page showing saved manga series in a grid/list format]*

#### Removing Series from Your Library

**Steps:**
1. Open your Library
2. Press it will go straight to your Detail page
3. Tap the **"Remove"** or **Delete** option
4. Confirm the removal

![Remove from Library - Placeholder](https://blairydev.github.io/ase456-individual-project.io/images/search.jpeg/images/detail-added.jpeg)
*[IMAGE: Screenshot showing the remove/delete option for a library item]*

#### Exporting Your Library

Save your library data to a file for backup or transfer.

**Steps:**
1. Go to Library Settings or Options menu
2. Tap **"Export Library"**
3. The app will save your library to your device's Downloads folder as `manga_export.json`
4. Use this file to backup your collection

![Export Library Option - Placeholder](https://blairydev.github.io/ase456-individual-project.io/images/search.jpeg/images/settings.jpeg)
file will be found on your phone Downloads folder

#### Importing Your Library

Restore your library from a previously exported file.

**Steps:**
1. Go to Settings or Options menu
2. Tap **"Import Library"**
3. Select the `.json` file from your device
4. The app will import all series that aren't already in your library

![Import Library Option - Placeholder](/images/settings.jpeg)
*[IMAGE: Screenshot showing the import library file picker]*

**Note:** Importing will not create duplicates. Only new series will be added.

## Troubleshooting

### Series Won't Load
**Problem:** The home page or series details aren't loading.

**Solutions:**
- Check your internet connection
- Pull down to refresh the page
- Close and reopen the app
- Verify that MangaDex is not experiencing downtime

### Chapters Won't Open
**Problem:** Tapping a chapter doesn't start the reader.

**Solutions:**
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection
- Try selecting a different chapter
- Return to the home page and navigate back to the series
- Restart the app

### Search Returns No Results
**Problem:** Search doesn't find any manga.

**Solutions:**
- Check your spelling
- Try searching with fewer or different keywords
- Use alternative titles or English/Japanese names
- Ensure your internet connection is active

### Library Not Saving
**Problem:** Added series don't appear in your library.

**Solutions:**
- Make sure you tapped "Add to Library"
- Check if the series is already in your library
- Restart the app
- Verify you have sufficient storage space

### Export/Import Failed
**Problem:** Unable to export or import library data.

**Solutions:**
- Ensure the app has storage permissions
- Check available storage space
- For import: Verify the file is a valid `manga_export.json` file
- Try exporting/importing again

## FAQ

### Q: Is the app free to use?
**A:** Yes! The Manga Reader App is free to use and connects to MangaDex's free manga database.

### Q: Do I need an account?
**A:** No account is required. Your library is stored locally on your device.

### Q: Can I read manga offline?
**A:** Currently, the app requires an internet connection to load and read chapters. Offline reading is not supported at this time.

### Q: How often are new series added?
**A:** The app pulls data from MangaDex, which is continuously updated by its community. New series and chapters are added daily.

### Q: Can I read in languages other than English?
**A:** The app currently displays chapters in English only. Multi-language support may be added in future updates.

### Q: How much storage does my library use?
**A:** Your library only stores text data (titles, descriptions, IDs). It uses minimal storage space, typically less than 1MB for hundreds of series.

### Q: What if a series I saved is removed from MangaDex?
**A:** The series will remain in your library, but chapters may not load if they're no longer available on MangaDex.

### Q: Can I sync my library across devices?
**A:** Use the Export/Import feature to transfer your library between devices manually. Automatic cloud sync is not currently available.

### Q: What content ratings are available?
